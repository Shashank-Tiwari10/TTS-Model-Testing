Console Table, Pendant Light, Mirror, aur Light Fixture ko theek se wipe kar lijiye, aur Door Mat ko achhe se jhaad dijiye taaki dust nikal jaaye. Master Bedroom mein Dressing Table, Nightstand, Sofa, Door, Side Table, aur Window ki achhe se Dusting karni hai, aur Mirror ko theek se wipe kar dijiye. Bed ka Make Bed kar lijiyega.
Phir Guest Bedroom Office mein Study Table ki achhe se Dusting kar dijiye.
Laundry Area mein Cloth Drying Stand ko theek se wipe kar lijiyega.
