Namaste. Aaj ki cleaning thoda detail mein bata rahi hoon, toh zara dhyaan se suniye.

Sabse pehle, dry work aur Dusting se shuru karte hain.
Foyer mein Door ki achhe se Dusting karni hai.
Phir Drawing Area mein Daybed aur Sofa ki theek se Dusting kar dijiye, aur Throw Pillow ka Fluff kar lijiye. Coffee Table ko achhe se wipe kar dijiyega.
Pool Area mein Wash Basin ko achhe se saaf karna hoga.
Uske baad, Dining Area mein Console Table, Door, Chair aur Window ki achhe se Dusting karni hai, aur Dining Chair, Dining Table aur Table ka Light Clean kar dijiye, bas ek quick wipe. Coffee Machine ko bhi achhe se wipe kar lijiyega.
Dining Washroom mein WC ko achhe se saaf kijiyega, aur Floor ka Thorough Clean karna hai, har side aur edges par wipe aur sanitise kar dijiyega.
Phir Living Area mein Sofa aur TV Unit ki achhe se Dusting karni hai, aur Throw Pillow ka Fluff kar lijiye. Cord ko theek se arrange kar dijiyega, aur Coffee Table ko achhe se wipe kar dijiye.
Master Bedroom mein Side Table, Window, Ceiling Fan aur Switchboard ki achhe se Dusting karni hai, aur Dressing Table aur Nightstand ka Light Clean kar dijiye, bas ek quick wipe. Ceiling par cobwebs dekhiyega, unhe hata dijiye aur halka Light Clean kar lijiye, aur Door aur Sofa ka Thorough Clean karna hoga, har side aur edges par wipe aur sanitise kijiyega. Mirror, Electonics Equipment, Lamp aur Light Fixture ko achhe se wipe kar dijiye, aur Area Rug ka Vacuum kar lijiye. Bed ka Change Sheets karna hai, aur Curtains ko achhe se jhaad dijiyega taaki dhool nikal jaaye.
Uske baad, Walk-in Closet mein Door ki achhe se Dusting karni hai, aur Ceiling par cobwebs dekh kar unhe hata dijiye aur halka Light Clean kar lijiye. Wardrobe aur Light Fixture ko achhe se wipe kijiyega.
Guest Bedroom Office mein Study Table ki achhe se Dusting kar dijiye.
Saath hi, Clothes ko paani se achhe se dho lijiyega.

Dusting ke baad, floor work karna hai.
Foyer aur Staircase 1F2F mein achhe se Sweep karke phir wet Mop laga dijiye.
Dining Area, Master Bedroom aur Walk-in Closet mein pehle Sweep karna hai aur phir sirf dry Mop, wahan wet Mop nahi lagega.

Phir outer areas ka kaam.
1F Balcony mein Window, Overhang Drawing Area aur Ceiling ka Light Clean kar dijiye, bas ek quick wipe, aur Floor ko paani se achhe se dho dijiyega. Railing aur Light Fixture ko achhe se wipe kar lijiye.
2F Balcony Front mein Overhang Bedroom aur Tree Debris ka Light Clean kar dijiye, bas ek quick wipe, aur Floor Sweep kijiyega, saari dhool utha lijiyega.

Ab Kitchen dekh lete hain.
Dry Kitchen mein Dining Chair ki achhe se Dusting karni hai, aur Island Counter aur Stove ya Cooktop ka Light Clean kar dijiye, bas ek quick wipe. Kitchen Sink ko achhe se saaf kijiyega, aur Backsplash, Chimney, Kitchen Countertop, Kitchen Cabinet (Lower), Kitchen Cabinet (Upper) aur Mixer ya Grinder ko achhe se wipe kar dijiye.
Wet Kitchen mein Kitchen Sink ko achhe se saaf karna hai, aur Utensild ko paani se achhe se dho lijiyega. Kitchen Countertop aur Microwave ko achhe se wipe kar dijiye.
Aur haan, ek aur baat, poore ghar ke liye zarurat ke hisaab se Toiletries Check refill kar dijiyega.
Aur haan, ek aur baat, poore ghar ki Water Bottles ko halka sa saaf karke refill kar dijiyega.

Washrooms mein —
Phir Master Bathroom mein Switchboard ki Dusting achhe se karni hai, aur Door, WC, Wash Basin, Floor, aur Glass Shower Partition ka Thorough Clean kar dijiye, sabhi sides aur edges ko wipe karke sanitise kijiyega. Mirror, Vanity Counter, Vanity Cabinet, Health Faucet, Shower Head, Wall Cabinet, Wooden Shelf, aur Light Fixture ko bhi theek se wipe kar lijiye.

Aur last mein, Client's Extra Work ka Add-on Work kar dijiye, aur Waste Management ka Manage bhi karna hoga.

Aaj ke liye bas itna hi hai. Sab kuch dhyaan se aur achhe se kijiyega. Dhanyavaad.
